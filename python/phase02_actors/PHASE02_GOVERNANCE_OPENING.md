## Purpose

Phase-02 establishes the **Actor and Role Model** for the kali-mcp-toolkit system. Actors are entities that interact with the system, and Roles define their permissions and responsibilities.

## Scope

1. **Actor Definitions** — Human, System, External entities
2. **Role Definitions** — Operator, Auditor, Administrator
3. **Permission Model** — What each role can do
4. **Actor Identity** — How actors are identified
5. **No Automated Actors** — All actors must be human-initiated
